◆ masterReset()

void DumbDisplay::masterReset ( )

EXPERIMENTAL; "master reset" to be just like uninitialized; "master reset" will: . disconnect from DD app (if connected) . delete all created layers and tunnels; hence, DO NOT use the pointers to them after "master reset" . DumbDisplay object will be just like at initial state; it will not be deleted

Since
0.9.8-r1
